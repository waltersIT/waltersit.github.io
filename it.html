As a proud member of the Ruxton community, Walter IT provides hands-on, personalized IT support to keep your technology running smoothly.   • Installing and configuring operating systems and applications.
  • Setting up and troubleshooting printers, scanners, and other peripherals.
  • Performing system cleanups and optimizing device performance.
  • Recovering lost or deleted files and data.
