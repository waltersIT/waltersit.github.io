Networking

Connecting Your World with Local Expertise

Walter IT specializes in building reliable and secure networks tailored to the needs of Ruxton homes and businesses. From setting up Wi-Fi systems that eliminate dead zones to configuring VPNs for remote work, we deliver efficient solutions with a personal touch. Avoid the frustration of long wait times and impersonal service—get network support from someone who’s right around the corner.

Walter IT ensures your home or business network is fast, reliable, and secure. Specific services include:

  • Setting up secure Wi-Fi networks and eliminating dead zones.
  • Installing and configuring routers, modems, and switches.
  • Creating and managing virtual private networks (VPNs) for remote work.
  • Troubleshooting internet connectivity and network performance issues.
  • Implementing guest networks and parental controls for added security.
