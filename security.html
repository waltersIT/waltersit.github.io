Security

Protecting What Matters Most with Care

Cybersecurity and disaster preparedness are vital, and Walter IT makes them accessible and affordable for the Ruxton community. We provide comprehensive security solutions, including firewall setup, intrusion detection, and phishing prevention, as well as robust disaster recovery plans, regular data backups, and failover systems to ensure business continuity. Unlike national services, we take the time to understand your unique concerns and offer tailored solutions to safeguard your data and operations.

Walter IT offers a full suite of security services to protect your systems and data. Specific services include:

  • Installing and maintaining antivirus and anti-malware software.
  • Setting up firewalls to block unauthorized access.
  • Configuring multi-factor authentication for secure logins.
  • Conducting vulnerability assessments and patching security gaps.
  • Implementing backup solutions and disaster recovery plans to safeguard data.
  • Monitoring systems for suspicious activity or potential breaches.
