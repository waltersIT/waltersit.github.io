Safety

Empowering Families and Businesses with Knowledge

Walter IT is dedicated to helping Ruxton parents and business owners protect their children and sensitive information in today’s digital landscape. We provide practical education on topics like managing parental controls, spotting online risks, and protecting against identity theft. With personalized workshops and one-on-one consultations, we empower you to keep your loved ones and data safe in a connected world.

Walter IT is committed to educating parents and business owners on protecting their families and sensitive information. Specific services include:

  • Teaching parents to set up and use parental controls on devices and apps.
  • Offering workshops on identifying and avoiding online risks like scams and phishing.
  • Advising on best practices for safeguarding children’s digital privacy.
  • Educating businesses on secure data handling and privacy compliance.
  • Providing one-on-one consultations to answer specific questions and concerns.
