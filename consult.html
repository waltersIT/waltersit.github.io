Consulting

Guiding Your Tech Journey, Locally and Affordably

Technology decisions are easier when you have a neighbor you can trust. Walter IT offers consulting services to help Ruxton businesses and families make informed IT choices. Whether you’re upgrading systems, transitioning to the cloud, or need guidance on IT budgeting, you’ll benefit from affordable, tailored advice from someone who understands your community’s needs.

Walter IT provides expert advice to help you make informed decisions about your technology needs. Specific services include:

  • Evaluating and recommending hardware upgrades or replacements.
  • Advising on software solutions for productivity, collaboration, or management.
  • Assisting with cloud migration and storage solutions.
  • Developing IT budgets and strategies to align with your goals.
  • Creating custom IT roadmaps for business growth and scalability.
