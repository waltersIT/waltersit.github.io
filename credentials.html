Walter in the Woods

Explore the Appalachian Trail through the eyes of Heisenberg, a trail name inspired by Breaking Bad and my own journey as Walter. This blog chronicles my adventure across 1,500 miles of breathtaking wilderness, spanning four unforgettable months. From scenic peaks to quiet forest paths, I share stories, photos, and lessons learned along the way. Dive into an interactive map, built with Leaflet, that traces my steps and showcases key locations along the trail. Whether you're a seasoned hiker, an aspiring adventurer, or just curious about life on the trail, Walter in the Woods offers a unique perspective on one of America’s most iconic journeys.
